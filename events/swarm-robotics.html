The coordination of relatively simple robots through the use of certain rules is the principle of Swarm Robotics. It takes its inspiration from societies of insects that can perform tasks that are beyond the capabilities of individuals. The honey bee's dance, the wasp's nest building, the construction of termite mounds, the trail following ants are wonders that never fail to fascinate us. Taking that swarming behavior we see around us every day and applying it to robotics is sure to be double the fun. Here's your opportunity to experience the thrill of developing and commanding a group of Swarm Robots with Hestia'20!
